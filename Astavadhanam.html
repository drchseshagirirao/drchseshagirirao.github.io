Astavadhanam

Avadhanam, is an intellectual discipline of highest order. This art refers to an ancient Indian skill, which apparently has been dying & there are very few living legends who can perform. It is an exercise of the mind, which demands simultaneously, range and focus. It subjects the Avadhani’s awareness; concentration, poetic excellence, retentive and analytical powers to the severest of tests.
